Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description:

Chocolate chip cookies are a classic American treat that combines the comforting richness of butter, sugar, and flour with the irresistible sweetness of semi-sweet chocolate chips. When baked, they offer a perfect balance of a crisp outer edge and a soft, chewy center, with each bite delivering a burst of melted chocolate. Whether enjoyed warm from the oven or at room temperature, these cookies are a timeless favorite for their simple yet indulgent flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup almond butter
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ teaspoon coarse salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a bowl, stir together almond butter, chocolate chips, sugar, eggs, and salt until a dough forms.
  2. Place 1-tablespoon mounds of dough 1 inch apart on parchment-lined baking sheets. Bake cookies until puffed and tops are set, about 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a wire rack; let cool. Cookies can be stored in an airtight container up to 3 days.
